SERVICE AGREEMENT

between
Northstar Studio (Pty) Ltd
and
Acme Field Services (Pty) Ltd

Effective date: 15 July 2026
Purpose. This agreement records the terms under which the Supplier will design, test, and maintain document-rendering services for the Client.

Clause 1 — Definitions

“Deliverables” means the templates, software, reports, and supporting material described in Schedule A. “Services” means the professional work required to produce and validate those Deliverables.

1.1 References to days mean business days unless expressly stated otherwise. Headings assist reading and do not limit interpretation.

Clause 2 — Appointment

The Client appoints the Supplier to perform the Services, and the Supplier accepts that appointment subject to this agreement. The Supplier will use reasonable skill, care, and diligence.

Clause 3 — Scope and change control

The Supplier will perform the work in Schedule A. Either party may propose a change in writing. No change affects price, timing, or responsibility until authorised representatives approve it.

3.1 A change request must describe the requested outcome, assumptions, dependencies, estimated effort, and effect on acceptance criteria.

Clause 4 — Client responsibilities

The Client will provide timely access to representative documents, lawful test data, decision makers, and the systems reasonably required for the Services.

Clause 5 — Fees and invoicing

Fees are calculated according to Schedule B. Undisputed invoices are payable within thirty days. Late payment does not waive the Supplier’s other rights.

Clause 6 — Acceptance

A Deliverable is accepted when it satisfies the agreed acceptance criteria or when the Client uses it in production, whichever occurs first. Rejection must identify reproducible non-conformities.

Clause 7 — Intellectual property

Each party retains its pre-existing materials. On full payment, the Client receives the rights expressly identified in Schedule A. General skills, methods, and non-confidential know-how remain with the Supplier.

Clause 8 — Confidentiality

Each recipient will protect confidential information with at least reasonable care, use it only for this agreement, and disclose it only to people who need it and are bound by equivalent duties.

Clause 9 — Personal information

Each party will process personal information lawfully and maintain appropriate safeguards. The Client will avoid supplying live personal information when representative synthetic data is sufficient.

Clause 10 — Security

The Supplier will follow documented access controls, report confirmed incidents without undue delay, and remove project credentials when they are no longer required.

Clause 11 — Warranties

Each party warrants that it has authority to enter this agreement. Except as expressly stated, all implied warranties are excluded to the extent permitted by law.

Clause 12 — Liability

Neither party is liable for indirect or consequential loss. Aggregate liability is limited to fees paid in the preceding twelve months, except where a limitation is prohibited by law.

Clause 13 — Indemnity

Each party will defend the other against third-party claims arising directly from its unlawful conduct, subject to prompt notice and reasonable control of the defence.

Clause 14 — Term and termination

This agreement begins on the effective date and continues for twelve months. Either party may terminate a material breach that remains uncured fourteen days after written notice.

Clause 15 — Exit assistance

On termination, the Supplier will provide paid, reasonable transition assistance and return or delete Client material according to written instructions and applicable retention duties.

Clause 16 — Dispute resolution

Representatives will first attempt good-faith negotiation. If unresolved after ten days, the dispute will be escalated to executives before court proceedings are started.

Clause 17 — General

Neither party may assign this agreement without consent, except to a successor of substantially all relevant business. Notices must be delivered to the addresses in Schedule C.

Clause 18 — Entire agreement

This document and its schedules form the entire agreement about the Services and replace prior proposals and discussions. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both parties.
